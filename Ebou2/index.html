Who We Are

A Business Built on Trust & Reliable Delivery

Gibba Logistics is a procurement and logistics company based in Banjul, The Gambia. We specialise in sourcing goods from international markets and coordinating delivery to clients across West Africa and beyond.

Our team has spent over a decade building a trusted network of verified suppliers across Asia, Europe, and the Americas. We handle everything — from a single personal order to full container loads — with transparent pricing and real-time updates every step of the way.

Our commitment is simple: get your goods to you on time, at a fair price, with zero hassle.
